Converse Collaborates With Three Emerging Designers On New Collection

The women's collection features designs from Koché, Feng Chen Wang, and Faith Connexion.

Converse has just announced a collaboration for their new women's statement collection featuring three young designers: Converse x Koché x Feng Chen Wang x Faith Connexion. The triple threat concept stems from reimagining of Converse through a lens focused on sport and utility. The resulting collection includes a comprehensive assortment of women's apparel, accessories, and footwear. Feng Chen Wang, Koché, and Faith Connexion began with two Converse footwear silhouettes rooted in the very DNA of the brand across basketball, court, and utility. The three creatives took inspiration from previously existing styles, including the iconic Chuck Taylor, ERX, Jack Purcell, the One Star, and others.

 

 

This is Converse's largest collaborative apparel offering to date and champions a unique expression of women's athletic wear. The brand manages to paint a new picture of itself within the world of streetwear as told by three exceptional visual storytellers. Converse by Koché x Feng Chen Wang x Faith Connexion will be available on April 26
